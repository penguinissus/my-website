ALL THE THINGS I LIKE

All the things I like is a playlist filled with jazz standards, mostly referencing The Real Book. The name "All the things I like" is a wordplay on my first favourite jazz chart - "All the Thing You Are"

BAND AND ORCH

Contains all of my favourite works for concert band and orchestra. Including works from composers like Samuel Hazo and the GOAT Stravinsky. BTW I currently play trombone and french horn in concerts band, jazz band, musical pit bands, and I used to play violin in orchestra.

EEPY zzzz

Everything I like from Studio Ghibili because Joe Hisaishi is the GOAT. This is just called eepy because this playlist contains all the sleepy music. I also play a lot of Studio Ghibili music with my quintet, it's our to go whenever we run out of ideas.

BIG BAND JAZZ

All the jazz charts I've played in jazz band and more. Has all the good stuff I've played from Gordon Goodwin and Charles Mingus. This playlist also includes the GOATs like Spain - Chick Corea, Sing Sing Sing, and TANK!

FINE SHYT

All the cantonese songs I listen to that reminds me of home, but they're not all on here because there's too much good cantonese music, so I decided to include one song from every goated singer I've listened to.
